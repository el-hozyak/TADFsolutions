Overview

Solution-processed OLEDs offer a cheaper fabrication technology, but the performance of these devices lags behind vacuum-deposited OLEDs, retarding their adoption. For SP-OLEDs to become widely adopted their performance must improve substantially. This requires both novel materials solutions and device solutions, underpinned by a fundamental understanding of the unique challenges facing the design and fabrication of these devices

We will produce new device models that address this issue through a dedicated and well-planned program that is based on targeted TADF emitter materials design
